## How to Use

1. **Open the Tool**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Add Your Courses**: Click "Add Course" and enter your course details
3. **Track Progress**: Check off topics as you complete them
4. **Monitor Analytics**: Use the Dashboard and Analytics tabs to track your progress
5. **Stay Motivated**: Watch your progress grow and maintain your study streak!
